The Light That Never Fades

Inspired by the spiritual teachings of Sant Shri Asharamji Bapuji

A humble lamp, when lit with sincerity, gives courage to every heart around it. In this gentle story, a simple act of faith becomes a beacon for those walking through confusion and darkness.

When fear grows strong, one person’s calm inner light can steady many. The lesson is clear: real strength is not in noise, but in the quiet courage to remain compassionate, truthful, and hopeful.

“A small light, when kept alive with devotion, can guide a whole path.”

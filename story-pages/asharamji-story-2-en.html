The River of Patience

Inspired by the spiritual teachings of Sant Shri Asharamji Bapuji

In a world that rushes toward answers, this story remembers the quiet strength of patience. A river does not hurry to reach the sea; it moves with calm continuity, shaping the land without force.

So too does the inner life grow when one learns to wait, observe, and trust. Patience becomes a way of living that softens anger, deepens wisdom, and brings clarity to the heart.

“The river teaches that grace is often found in steady movement, not in haste.”

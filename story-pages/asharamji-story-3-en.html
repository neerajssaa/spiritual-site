The Mountain and the Breeze

Inspired by the spiritual teachings of Sant Shri Asharamji Bapuji

Even the tallest mountain does not resist the breeze; it allows the air to pass and remain calm. In the same way, one who is rooted in wisdom can remain serene even when life’s currents become strong.

True strength lies in accepting what comes without losing one’s inner balance. A peaceful mind becomes a shelter not only for oneself but also for others who seek comfort.

“A steady heart can weather every storm without surrendering its peace.”
